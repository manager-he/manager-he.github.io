## ğŸ“ How to Customize

### 1. Replace Profile Picture
Replace `profile.jpg` with your own photo (recommended size: 250x250px or larger, square format)

### 2. Update Personal Information
Edit `index.html` and replace the following placeholders:

**About Section:**
- `Your Name` - Your full name
- `[Your Field of Study]` - Your field of study or research area
- `[Master's/Ph.D.]` - Your degree program
- `[University Name]` - Your university name
- `[Brief description...]` - Your research focus

**Education Section:**
- Update degree information, universities, dates, GPA, advisors, etc.

**Research Section:**
- Add your research interests
- Update project titles and descriptions
- Add your publications (use proper citation format)

**Contact Section:**
- Replace email address: `your.email@university.edu`
- Update office location
- Add your social media links (GitHub, LinkedIn, Google Scholar)
- Link to your CV PDF

### 3. Customize Colors (Optional)
Edit `styles.css` to change colors:
- Primary color: `#007bff` (blue)
- Dark text: `#2c3e50`
- Light backgrounds: `#f8f9fa`
